# human_sexavg_RR_Bherer2017

Genome-wide recombination rate data from Bherer et al. 2017.
Sex-averaged recombination rates for GRCh37/hg19 across the human
genome.

## Format

A data.table with columns:

- chr:

  Chromosome number

- pos:

  Base pair position

- rate:

  Recombination rate (cM/Mb)

- cM:

  Cumulative genetic map position (centiMorgans)

## Source

Bherer et al. (2017) - refined maps from pedigree and population data
